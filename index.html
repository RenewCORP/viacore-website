Our Advisory Solutions

Our advisory model integrates asset data, lifecycle strategy, risk, compliance, sustainability, and operational performance — providing clarity at the core of Strategic Asset Management.

Asset Advisory

Accurate, structured asset information to underpin confident decision-making.

  • Asset validation and field verification
  • Condition assessment (physical, operational & risk aligned)
  • ISO 55000 alignment and maturity benchmarking
  • Asset register optimisation
  • CMMS/EAM data optimisation

Operational Risk & Contract Advisory

Strengthening FM governance, regulatory compliance and service performance.

  • Technical due diligence
  • FM contract mobility and transition assessments
  • Property and asset regulatory compliance reviews

Lifecycle Strategy & Capital Planning

Supporting long-term asset performance and portfolio-level risk management.

  • 10–20 year CapEx and OpEx financial planning
  • Capital prioritisation frameworks
  • Strategic Asset Management Plans (SAMPs)
  • Long-term asset renewal strategies

Sustainability, Energy & ESG
